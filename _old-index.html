What is COP27 Conference?

Egypt is hosting the 27th UN Climate Change Conference of the Parties (COP27) in Sharm El Sheikh on 13-17 November 2022.

The COP27 will bring parties and stakeholders together to accelerate action towards the goals of the Paris Agreement and the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC).
