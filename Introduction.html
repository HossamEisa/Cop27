Introduction

Facing Climate Change Requires Bold, Rapid and Collective Actions

Globally, the escalating number of extreme weather events is impacting the life and livelihood of millions of people. Unpredictable weather and rapid global warming are causing alarming risks to human beings and all other forms of life on Earth.

Our collective efforts to combat the adverse impacts of climate change is mandatory to secure sustainable future for all. Effective and coordinated climate action has resulted in cleaner air, green jobs, restored natural resources and tapped upon new economic potential.

We should act immediately to counter this raging climate crisis, and together strengthen the implementation of climate action to ensure a resilient planet.

COP27 will include 13 days of negotiations – with the participation of almost 200 countries or Parties.

The aim of Egypt’s COP27 Presidency is to ensure fulfilling commitments of COP26 decisions and outcomes. Furthermore, championing a breakthrough in adaptation, mitigation and climate financing will be of the utmost importance to developing countries in the fight against climate change.

We need to harmonize our global efforts, turning words into action but that will only be achieved if every country delivers on pledged commitments and raises their ambition.
