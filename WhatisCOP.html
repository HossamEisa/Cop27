What is A COP?

A defining moment for Egypt and Africa’s leadership role in the fight against climate change.

In November 2022, Egypt will host the 27th UN Climate Change Conference of the Parties (COP27) in Sharm El Sheikh. This event is known by many to be a monumental occasion to address the increasing pressures of climate change.

COP27 is the 2022 United Nations Climate Change Conference

Since 1992, the UN has been organizing global climate conferences for 197 Parties – that have signed and agreed to the terms and conditions of the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC). Therefore, these global climate conferences were coined as ‘Conference of the Parties’ or COPs.

Since that time, the issue of collectively addressing climate change has become more vital and necessary with climate as the most intense multilateralism fora.

This year will be the 27th annual climate conference – giving it the name COP27.

Egypt will serve as President, and COP27 will be held in Sharm El Shiekh.

COP27 will be decisive in operationalizing and implementing the goals set out in the Paris Agreements and the UNFCCC.
