Prime Minister Speech

H.E President Abdel Fattah ElSisi, the Government and the people of Egypt, are proud of the acknowledgment of the International Community that Egypt has earned to host the COP27, in Sharm El Sheikh, in November 2022.

While understanding the challenges, we in Egypt are confident that in Sharm EL Sheikh, the World is determined to secure out of the Conference a universal success story cemented with the international communityâ€™s appreciation that we are gathering in Sharm not just to address one of the crises that the world is facing, rather than considering an eminent climate dangers that could threaten the human security of the world and a looming crisis on the future of the planet on which we live and hope to prosper.

Egypt recognizes that the Conference will be convened under troubling and serious crises in the world, which add to the challenges of the very issues of the Conference. However, we trust that the countries of the world realize that they are addressing in Sharm a crisis of its own kind with lasting dangers, and that a delayed or insufficient cooperative solutions, would bring the whole world closer to devastating damages and losses, and possibly unaffordable human and financial costs, particularly on the developing countries, and most particularly the African Countries among them.

We are confident, therefore, that a prevailing sense of responsibility equal to the seriousness of the issues of the Conference, would be shared among the Egyptâ€™s Presidency of the Conference and all participating countries, as well as the civil society, youth and the private sector, that will enable the Conference to attain common solutions that are fairly implementable and avail the world a strategic exit to a safer world.

Needless to say, that a successful Conference in Sharm El Sheikh, will provide the Multilateral System a much needed and rare supportive opportunity, at a time, when the system is being subjected to tremendous pressures that are weakening its basic global tools to maintain the international peace and security, and therefore, we pray that from Egypt we will bring to the world a consolidated message of peace and credibility of the international cooperation within an effective the United Nations System.

In conclusion, the Peace City of Sharm ELSheikh, and its citizens feel fortunate to receiving all participating delegations with the promise to make their visit and stay flavored with pleasure and warmth.

Dr. Mostafa Kamal Madbouli

Prime Minister of Egypt
