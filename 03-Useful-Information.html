Useful Information

Visas

Visas for Egypt are obtained upon arrival into the territory or through the Egyptian embassy or consulate. All delegates must complete the online form found below to ensure visa approval in in both cases.

Accommodation

We would advise all delegates to book their accommodation for COP27 as soon as possible. Sharm El Sheikh provides a range of accommodation options that ensure an enriching and comfortable experience with a low environmental footprint.

To book your stay at one of Sharm El Sheikh’s sustainable hotels, please access the link below.

Book Your Hotel Now

Flights

Cairo International Airport and Sharm El Sheikh International Airport offer a host a multitude of direct flights and a flexible schedule to guarantee a smooth journey to attend COP27.

EGYPTAIR, the national airline of Egypt and Member of Star Alliance, proudly welcomes you onboard its flights to attend COP27. As the official carrier of this global event, EGYPTAIR offers you special flights’ fares to Sharm El- Sheikh accessible through the following link:

Explore EGYPTAIR Offers

Transportation

Sharm El Sheikh is served by a well-connected, and extended network of sustainable transport systems. The main COP venue will be served by its own electric bus station that will operate daily shuttle buses in hotspots across the city. We are working to ensure a range of affordable, and sustainable modes of transport.
