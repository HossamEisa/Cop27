The COP Presidency.

Egypt is hosting the Presidency for COP27.

To accelerate global climate actions through actual implementation, support emissions reductions in all sectors, and enhance adaptation to the impacts of climate change, a just transition is needed to allow us to fulfill the development human needs of all countries.

This will be achieved through the following priorities:

  1. Ensuring progress towards the Global Goal on Adaptation and fulfilling adaptation pledges.
  2. Raising ambition through the submission of updated WOC and its potential support.
  3. Accelerate fulfillment of climate finance and its modality by governments, international organizations and the private sector.
  4. Collaborating through a multistakeholder process that is inclusive, to showcase successful climate actions at all fronts, with a focus on establishing resilient human livelihoods to counter the climate crisis.
