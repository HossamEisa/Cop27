Become a Sponsor

The presidency programme for COP27 showcased momentum from the whole of society, and focused on key issues to drive ambition and action.

Be a Sponsor at COP27 Egypt

The Government of Egypt is offering a unique opportunity for business organizations to be an official sponsor and partner to the Egyptian presidency of COP27.

Support the success of the most important annual climate summit by:

  1. Contributing to enhance awareness of climate change key challenges
  2. Providing financial resources and technical expertise before and during the conference
  3. Inspiring other businesses to take actions on the climate agenda and set ambitious net-zero targets

By partnering with COP27 Egyptian Presidency, your organization will benefit from:

  1. Reaffirming your position as a leader on climate action
  2. Unrivalled networking opportunities with governments and global business leaders
  3. Opportunity to host side events and exhibitions as part of the conference
  4. Promotion of your climate ambitions and projects within the venue and through COP27 communication channels

For any inquiries please contact us sponsorship@cop27.eg
