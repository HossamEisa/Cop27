Sharm El Sheikh International Convention Center (SHICC) is one of the largest and most innovative conferences centers in the Middle East and Africa. The SHICC can be reached within minutes from Sharm El Sheikh International Airport and boasts advanced facilities and provides a host of technical services.

Established in 2006, the SHICC was designed to accommodate the regionâ€™s most prominent events including the World Youth Forum (WYF), Conference of the States Parties to the United Nations Convention against Corruption (UNODC) and the Conference of the Parties to the Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD COP14).

Overall, the conference complex has a variety of flexible conference and exhibition halls that will form the Blue Zone for COP27.

The Green Zone will be opposite the conference center and serve as an active space for youth groups, civil society, academia, artists, business from across Egypt, Africa and all over the world to host events, exhibitions, cultural performances, workshops and talks.
